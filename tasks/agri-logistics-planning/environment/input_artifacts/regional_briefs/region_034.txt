REGIONAL MISSION BRIEF: region_034

REGION: Gambela

ZONE: Anywak

------------------------------

Market access in Anywak is improving with the recent completion of the secondary road network, although high transport tariffs continue to dampen the profit margins of smallholder farmers in the Gambela region.

Temperature anomalies of +1.5°C have been recorded in Anywak, leading to increased evapotranspiration rates. This has prompted local agronomy leads to recommend early-maturing varieties for the upcoming cycle.

Technical assessments of Wheat yield in Anywak suggest an average productivity of 2.8 tons per hectare, which is slightly above the five-year mean for the Gambela region. Maize performance, however, remains vulnerable to Striga infestation.

Warehouse inspections in Anywak reveal mixed integrity levels. Hub A-12 remains operational at 90% capacity, while secondary storage units in Gambela require immediate roof repairs before the rainy season commences.
