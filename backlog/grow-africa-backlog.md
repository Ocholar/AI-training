# GROW-Africa SwarmBench Backlog: Planning and Operations (v2.1)

This backlog contains 30 high-fidelity Planning and Operations task candidates derived from the GROW-Africa database. Every task focuses on resource allocation, logistics optimization, or operational risk management.

---

## Cluster A: Resource Distribution and Logistics (10 Tasks)
Goal: Optimize the flow of agricultural inputs and outputs under constraints.

1.  [Planning] Ethiopia Highlands Seed Restoration: Design a shipment schedule to deliver 1,000t of maize hybrid seeds to 20 sub-performing L3 zones.
2.  [Operations] Sub-Saharan Fertilizer Routing: Optimize a 5,000-ton Urea distribution plan from port nodes to L3 hubs with > 2t/ha yield gaps.
3.  [Planning] Cold Chain Hub Placement: Select top 10 locations for climate-controlled stores based on "harvest density" clusters from Point.csv.
4.  [Operations] Last-Mile Input Deployment: Finalize a multi-modal schedule for delivering agro-chemicals to 40 remote "GADM_ID" nodes.
5.  [Operations] Emergency Drought Grain Dispatch: Rank 50 regions by "Yield Deficit to Need Ratio" and plan immediate relief distribution.
6.  [Logistics] Port-to-Farm Connectivity Optimization: Map and resolve logistics bottlenecks for DAP fertilizer from Djibouti port into the Oromia highlands.
7.  [Operations] Warehouse Inventory Reconciliation: Match 50 technical regional briefs with production forecasts to detect "stock-out" risks.
8.  [Planning] Cooperative Aggregation Strategy: Group 100 L3 smallholder farms into 5 "High-Volume Pickup Hubs" for optimal bulk buyer logistics.
9.  [Logistics] Multi-Modal Fertilizer Relay: Coordination of rail-to-truck transfer for Urea distribution across 40 L1 administrative boundaries.
10. [Operations] Fuel-Efficient Collection Routes: Design optimal pickup sequences for 20 cooperatives to minimize transport costs per ton.

## Cluster B: Storage, Capacity and Infrastructure (10 Tasks)
Goal: Manage storage assets and infrastructure structural constraints.

11. [Planning] Grain Bank Capacity Scaling: Identify 15 regions where harvest-cut production peaks exceed local storage by > 20%.
12. [Operations] Infrastructure Constraint Audit: Reconcile 50 structural safety briefs (e.g., bridge collapses, hub damage) with distribution maps.

14. [Operations] Pest-Resistant Hub Selection: Filter distribution hubs based on moisture/pest risk profiles buried in regional technical prose.
15. [Operations] Buffer Stock Management: Plan "safety stock" levels for 40 zones based on multi-year yield volatility stats.
16. [Planning] Cross-Border Trading Nodes: Identify optimal LG GIDs for export/import nodes based on transit-time analytics from Point data.
17. [Operations] Packaging and Handling Logistics: Calculate required 50kg sack inventory for 20 districts based on "Production_kg" forecasts.
18. [Planning] Agricultural Park Zoning: Define "High-Intensity zones" for specialized equipment sharing based on production density.
19. [Operations] Seasonal Labor Distribution: Plan the movement of harvest teams to match "CropCut" timelines across varying climate zones.
20. [Planning] Water Hub Distribution: Optimize the delivery of mobile irrigation units to L3 clusters with high survey-reported drought stress.
